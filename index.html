learn-clojure

A ring Compojure based web site devoted to teaching and promoting Clojure and Clojurescript. The idea here is to provide the information so that aspiring Clojure programmers can use the structure of the site as well as the resources to quickly get their application off the ground. A further goal is to use Clojurescript and explore the new paradigm of using Clojure and Clojurescript together to eliminate the extra complexity that ajax, json and rest sometimes add to projects

Usage

lein ring server-headless
